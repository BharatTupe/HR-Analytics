# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze workforce data and provide actionable insights into employee attrition, workforce demographics, departmental performance, salary distribution, and employee experience.

The dashboard enables HR teams and business stakeholders to identify the major factors contributing to employee attrition, monitor workforce trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

# Business Problem

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges for organizations. Losing skilled employees increases recruitment costs, training expenses, and affects overall productivity.

This dashboard helps HR departments answer questions such as:

- Which departments have the highest employee attrition?
- Which salary groups experience the highest attrition?
- Which job roles are most affected?
- How does employee satisfaction impact attrition?
- Which age groups have the highest number of employees?
- What is the overall workforce distribution?
- Which departments have the largest workforce?
- How does employee experience relate to attrition?

# Key Insights

Some important business insights generated from the dashboard include:

- Employee attrition rate is approximately **16.3%**.
- Operations department has the largest workforce.
- Administration department contributes the highest employee attrition.
- Employees in the **6–10 LPA** salary range experience the highest attrition.
- Most employees belong to the **26–35 years** age group.
- Male employees represent a larger percentage of the workforce.
- Certain job roles show lower job satisfaction and higher attrition, helping HR identify areas for improvement.
- Employee attrition is higher among employees with lower years of experience.
